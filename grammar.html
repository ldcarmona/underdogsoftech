What is a gerund?

A gerund is a form that is derived from a verb but functions as a noun. Gerunds can be easily mistaken for a verb. Learning about gerunds can be tricky if you don't pay attention or use them incorrectly which causes more confusion. This video is a brief introduction to Gerunds and its function.
